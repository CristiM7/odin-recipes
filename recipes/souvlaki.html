Gyros Souvlaki

The Tzatziki Beast

Souvlaki, marinated meat grilled on a skewer, is a Greek fast food. Pork is traditionally used to make the dish, but chicken, lamb, and beef are also common

You can enjoy souvlaki straight off the skewer as a kebab, in a warm pita with your favorite toppings, or over a bed of fresh veggies

Ingredients

  • 1 pork tenderloin, cut into strips
  • 1 clove of garlic, chopped
  • 1 or 2 red onion, sliced
  • 3 or 4 tbsps of olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix into a large bowl the tenderloin (cut into strips), the garlic, the red onion, 3-4 tbsp of olive oil, 1-2 tbsp of red wine vinegar, some thyme, some rosemary, and mix
  2. In a (very hot) large non-sticking pan, add the tenderloin along with the ingredients of the marinade, and saut√© for 1 minute on each side
  3. To prepare the tzatziki sauce for the pork gyros, pour in a food processor the olive oil and garlic and blend until the garlic is diluted
