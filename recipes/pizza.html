Pizza Quattro Formaggi

A delicious pizza with four types of cheese

Quattro Formaggi pizza is made from four types of cheese

Other than the mozzarella, three typical kinds of cheese paired into the pie are; gorgonzola, fontina, and Parmigiano-Reggiano

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Passata
  • Mozzarella and goat cheese
  • Pepper
  • Parmezan and Gorgonzola

Steps

  1. First, put the flour, salt and yeast in a bowl making sure to put the salt and yeast at opposite sides
  2. Add the water and olive oil and mix together to form a rough dough
  3. Shape the dough into a ball then knead on a lightly floured surface for around 10 minutes until the dough is smooth and elastic
  4. After kneading shape the dough into a ball and place in a large clean bowl lightly greased with olive oil. C
  5. Once the dough has risen, knock the air out by pressing down on it with your fist. Divide in half and shape into two separate balls ready for baking
  6. Bake the pizza in a pre-heated oven until golden, bubbling and melted. Let it sit for 5 minutes before cutting
